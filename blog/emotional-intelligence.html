Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is the ability to recognise and understand your own emotions as well as others. We can use this awareness to guide our thoughts and behaviour. Having emotional intelligence helps manage our emotions and to respond effectively to the emotions of other people. EI is important because it helps us to build stronger relationships. Communicate effectively. Manage stress and conflicts better. Helps us to make better decisions and also achieve greater success and well-being. emotional intelligence can be developed through practice and experiences. Some strategies for developing EI include:
-mindfulness and self reflection
-engaging in active listening
-learning to manage stress and negative emotions
-seeking feedback and constructive criticism

How is EQ Different to IQ?

EQ and IQ are definite measures of human abilities. The main differences are:

IQ: focuses on cerebral abilities, problem-solving and reasoning, and thinking speed and also memory. IQ is measured through standardized tests. IQ also anticipates academic achievements and to some extent professional success.
EQ: mainly focuses on emotional awareness, social skills and relationships. It anticipates success in interpersonal relationships, leaderships and teamwork. Can be developed through practice, training and experiences.

Why Is EQ Important?

EQ can be seen as a key factor in personal and professional success. It can help us to build stronger relationships, have compassion and manage our emotions/self regulate so whenever we do get frustrated we don't lash out on someone else, instead we should find ways to calm ourselves; regulate our emotions and revisit the problem. EQ also helps us to have empathy, keeps us motivated using emotions to achieve goals. It also helps us to build social skills; this applies emotional awareness and understanding to interact and communicate effectively with other people. Humans are social beings, so it makes sense to practice EQ. Not only this helps us to have better relationship with others, it also aids us to have a better understanding and helps us to be nicer to ourselves.
