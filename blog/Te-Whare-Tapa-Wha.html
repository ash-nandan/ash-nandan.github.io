      WALL ACTIVITIES
      Physical Well-Being Going to the gym 3-4 times per week
      Family Well-Being Having dinner and watching a movie together most evenings
      Spiritual Well-Being I've never known how to take care of myself spiritually but I tried something new this year which was writing my thoughts and feelings down. I was never a big fan of such thing and I don't even know if it actually helped me. Not 100% sure how I feel about journaling. Though, I'm guessing there is another way to take care of myself spiritually. I just need to find it.
      Emotional and Mental Well-Being Isolating myself in my room or a space where it's just me and my headphones and music. I also like to do arts and crafts which surprisingly has helped me calm down and relax.
      Whenua Few times a year whenever I get the chance. I take myself far away somewhere like on a long road trip (I enjoy road trips a lot but hardly ever get to do it) I go somewhere peaceful and quiet and with less people around with no noise. Somwhere I can actually hear my inner thoughts and to feel connected to nature and myself. It helps me forget my stresses and worries for a moment. This also helps me to clear my head.
